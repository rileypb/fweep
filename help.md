# Fweep Help

## Navigating the Map

You can move around the map by middle-clicking and dragging, or by single-clicking and dragging on the minimap in the upper right corner.

---

## Creating, Editing and Deleting Rooms

You can create a room by double-clicking on an empty space on the map. This will bring up the room editor, in which you can name your room, set its description, and change its style and shape. You may dismiss the editor by hitting escape, clicking the X button, or clicking outside of the editor on the map.

Double-clicking on a room also shows the room editor.

Single-clicking on a room selects it. You can then delete the room with the backspace or delete key.

---

## Creating, Editing and Deleting Connections

You can create a connection between two rooms by clicking on one of the directional handles that appear when you hover over the source room, and dragging to the desired handle on the target room. 

Dragging to the center of the target room instead of to a handle creates a one-way connection, represented with arrowheads.

Double-clicking on a connection brings up the connection editor, which allows you to change the style and labels on the connection.

The start label and end label are displayed at the start and end of a connection respectively.

The annotation chooses special rendering for the connection, including free-form text if desired.

Selecting a connection and hitting backspace or delete will delete the connection.

---

## Other controls

### Undo/Redo

These buttons can be found in the lower right corner, or you can use CTRL-Z and CTRL-Y to undo and redo respectively.

### Prettify 

The prettify button (represented with a flower) attempts to arrange the graph in a pleasing manner, as much as possible.

### Snap to Grid

By default, moving a room causes it to snap to the grid coordinates. You can turn this behavior off by clicking the "waffle" button in the lower right corner.

### Dark Mode/Light Mode

These can be toggle using the button in the lower right corner.

### Grid Display

This can be toggled on/off using the grid icon in the upper left corner.
